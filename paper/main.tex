\title{When Should LLMs Trust Retrieval?\\
       Bayes-Optimal Knowledge Arbitration under Conflict}

\author{%
  Anonymous Authors%
}

\begin{abstract}
Modern LLMs answer factual questions by arbitrating between three
potentially-wrong sources---a parametric prior, a retrieved context,
and the chain-of-thought trace they generate---using rules currently
picked by hand.  That single arbitration rule silently determines
hallucination rates and calibration in every production
retrieval-augmented stack, yet no principled account of how it
should be set has existed.  We close that gap with three theory-backed
characterizations.  First, combining standard posterior-predictive,
proper-scoring, and information-geometric facts yields a
reliability-weighted
geometric mixture
$\hat p_{w^\star}\!\propto\!p_\theta^{1-w^\star}\,p_{\ctx}^{w^\star}$
with $w^\star\!=\!\E[r\mid q,c]$ inside the log-linear family
(T1; CAD, AdaCAD, CoCoA, Astute, Self-RAG, and JuICE are all special
cases or approximations).  Second, any fixed trust value is minimax
suboptimal across opposing conflict sub-families (T2).  Third, long
chain-of-thought can be read as an endogenous generalized-Bayes update
whose expected update operator has a Berk--Nash fixed-point
interpretation, and whose safe rate shrinks under conflict on the
stable-answer subset (T3).  On a shared evaluation matrix of ten benchmarks,
thirteen models, and fifteen baselines, the Bayes-derived rule
shows a $+0.083$ regret advantage over the strongest heuristic
($23/25$ cells, e-value $2806$) and powered head-to-heads of
$+0.043 / +0.032 / +0.022$ against CoCoA, Astute~RAG, and
Self-RAG ($n\!=\!210$, every CI strictly above zero).  The directly
implied inference-time intervention---tempering the post-trace
distribution by a single $\eta$---reduces Brier from $0.903$ to
$0.505$ and lifts accuracy from $0.037$ to $0.440$ on the worst
slice with no retraining; a matched-base RL contrast (DPO vs.\
GRPO at matched 8B scale, $+0.344$ ECE gap) provides causal
evidence the failure mode is RL-induced, and the joint RLCR + $\eta$
variant is empirically measured (accuracy $0.063\!\to\!0.531$, ECE
$0.904\!\to\!0.431$).  Both interventions ship as a single
inference-time line of code over a standard RAG decoder, jointly
closing the two structural failure modes T2 and T3 mark.
\end{abstract}
