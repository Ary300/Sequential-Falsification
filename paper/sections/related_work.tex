\section{Related Work}
\label{sec:related}

The conflict framing originates with~\citet{longpre2021entity}; surveys carve sub-types~\citep{xu2024knowledgeconflicts,xie2024conflictqa}, and benchmarks span synthetic counter-memory~\citep{su2024conflictbank}, real Wikipedia contradictions~\citep{hou2024wikicontradict}, temporal drift~\citep{marjanovic2024dynamicqa}, and popularity stratification~\citep{mallen2023popqa}. Adaptive RAG variants threshold on popularity, learn when to retrieve~\citep{asai2024selfrag,jiang2023flare}, correct retrieval errors~\citep{yan2024crag}, or debate documents~\citep{wang2024astuterag,wang2025ramdocs}. None derives the trust schedule from a posterior over reliability; survey work flags fixed-trust as suboptimal but stops short of the optimum, which we identify as $w^\star\!=\!\E[r\!\mid\!s]$ (\Cref{thm:bayes-arbitration}).

At the decoder level, CAD~\citep{shi2024cad}, AdaCAD~\citep{wang2025adacad}, CoCoA~\citep{khandelwal2025cocoa}, JuICE~\citep{li2025taming}, and contrastive decoding~\citep{li2023contrastive} reweight contextual logits with hand-crafted signals. Each sits at a fixed point on the same exponent axis as our minimizer (\Cref{cor:special-cases-body}); none is interpreted by its authors as Bayes-optimal arbitration, and none carries a perturbation envelope or a closed-form readout-time correction.

The reasoning-time calibration literature reads as mutually contradictory: \citet{kadavath2022,yoon2025confidence} report calibrated reasoning on standard QA, while \citet{halawi2024overthinking,lacombe2025dontthinktwice,welch2026cost,damani2025rlcr,bereket2025grpo,sun2025context} document overthinking, implicit answer-conditioning, and RL-induced overconfidence on knowledge-intensive tasks. The split has one root cause: no theory tells the practitioner whether the cell sits below or above the safe-Bayes learning rate. \Cref{thm:eta-bayes} reconciles the two camps and \Cref{cor:phase-boundary} gives the dimensional threshold $\eta_c\!=\!1/D^\star$. RLHF~\citep{christiano2017rlhf,ouyang2022instructgpt}, DPO~\citep{rafailov2023dpo}, and GRPO~\citep{shao2024grpo} shape the post-trained distribution differently~\citep{pan2022reward}; our matched-base contrast at fixed Llama-3.1-8B base isolates the recipe-level calibration effect from confounders. Weight-editing~\citep{meng2022rome,meng2023memit} is an orthogonal complement.

On the opinion-pool axiomatics side, \citet{mcconway1981marginalization} and \citet{genest1986characterization} characterize geometric pooling under marginalization and external Bayesianity, both of which pin $w$ to a constant. \Cref{thm:t1-axiomatic} relaxes EB to log-likelihood-ratio invariance, which permits context-dependent $w$, exactly what RAG arbitration needs. We transport classical results to the LLM CoT setting without reproving them: Berk's KL-projection concentration~\citep{berk1966}, Kleijn--van der Vaart~\citep{kleijn2006}, Bissiri--Holmes--Walker generalized Bayes~\citep{bissiri2016}, Gr\"unwald--van Ommen safe-Bayes~\citep{grunwald2017}, and endogenous-evidence Berk--Nash dynamics~\citep{esponda2016,fudenberg2021}; the spectral diagnostic in CT2 is the first observable rate in this line.
