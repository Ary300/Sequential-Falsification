\section{Discussion}
\label{sec:discussion}

\paragraph{Conclusion.}
Treating arbitration as parameter estimation on one free-energy functional yields the combiner, the trajectory envelope, and the deployment scalar from a single object. CT1 pins the unique geometric mixture $\hat p_{w^\star}\!\propto\!p_\theta^{1-w^\star}p_\ctx^{w^\star}$ subsuming CAD, AdaCAD, CoCoA, Astute~RAG, Self-RAG, and JuICE; CT2 makes the chain-of-thought rate $\widehat\rho^\star$ observable; CT3 gives the closed-form Brier improvement $(1\!-\!\eta^\star)^{2}V_{\mathrm{post}}$. Empirically: powered $n\!=\!210$ separates BayesArbiter at $+0.064/+0.043/+0.032/+0.022$ vs.\ AdaCAD/CoCoA/Astute/Self-RAG, $\widehat\rho^\star$ predicts saturation at $r\!=\!0.997$, RLCR-trained 8B worst cell moves $0.063\!\to\!0.531$ accuracy and $0.886\!\to\!0.415$ Brier under $\eta$-tempering, and the matched-base GRPO-vs-DPO $+0.344$ gap across five families gives the first causal evidence that a post-training recipe induces context-overtrust.

\paragraph{Limitations and future work.}
The matched-base recipe claim rests on a Llama-3.1-8B anchor with cross-family replications (Phi-3, Mistral, Gemma); a from-scratch base outside Llama lineage would harden it. Qwen2.5-32B / ConflictBank slip into an absolute-confidence floor regime outside the CT2 linear sandwich (App.~\ref{app:limitations}); $K\!=\!3$ multi-source is synthetic; experiments are English-only; frontier validation uses top-$k$ logprobs. Natural extensions: $K$-hop agentic composition (\Cref{thm:t15-compositional}), doubly-robust causal $w^\star$ identification (\Cref{thm:t16-causal}), and adversarial robustness via the Stackelberg equilibrium of $\mathcal{F}$ inside CT1$'$.
